APPETIZER

An appetizer is a small dish served before a meal to stimulate the appetite. It can be hot or cold, and usually consists of a savory item, such as cheese, meat or vegetables. Common appetizers include bruschetta, shrimp cocktail, deviled eggs, and nachos. The purpose of an appetizer is to whet the appetite and prepare the palate for the main course. It is often served at parties and restaurants as a way to start the dining experience on a delicious note.
